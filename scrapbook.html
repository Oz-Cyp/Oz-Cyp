TURKISH CYPRIOT BARBEQUE CULTURE

Also known as: 'MANGAL'




for 'Turkish text' press: 9

A 'MANGAL' event can be dividied into 4 subdivisions:

  • 1. Preperations

    • Preparation is the most crucial part since it determines the quality of the food. Quality of the meat you buy, coal you burn appetizers ... you name it.
    Usually man prepare the skewers while woman is busy with salad, rice houmus, etc..

    Figure above: Here we can see a happy Cypirot mom preparing the green, knowing she will eventually eat kebab!


  • 2. Cooking

    • Mangal time ! Grab your raki, whisky, beer or any type of alcohol and procede with barbeque.




  • 3. Eating

    • Nothing to explain here. But here are some of the food that you can see as 'Mangal Cousine' on the table:

    Appetizers Main Food
    Tazziki Şeftali Kebab
    billurcux Lamb Skewers
    houmus Lamb Chops
    Halloumi Baugette Kofte

  • 4. Afterparty

    • The worst part of the event as usually some unncesarily nostalgic people grab their guitars to play traditional songs, one of which always have to be Meditarranean Sundance by Haluk Levent.
